Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 16, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information that Flappy Planes collects, uses, and shares when you use the app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

Persistent Data: Our game uses internal storage to save game progress and settings. No personal information is collected or stored.
Third-Party Services: We do not integrate any third-party services that collect data.
3. How We Use Your Information

Game Progress and Settings: The data stored internally is only used to save and restore your game progress and settings. It is not shared with any third parties.

4. Sharing Your Information

No Sharing: We do not share any information with third parties, as we do not collect or transmit any personal data.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate steps to ensure the security of the data stored on your device. This includes implementing technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your data.

6. Your Rights

Access, Correction, and Deletion: Since we do not collect personal information, you do not have the right to access, correct, or delete such information.
Complaints: If you have any concerns about how your data is handled, you can contact us directly.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any significant changes will be communicated to you through the app.
